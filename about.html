Research interests include parameterized algorithms, structural graph theory, applied discrete mathematics, random graphs, parallel algorithm design, and combinatorial scientific computing. Recent work focuses on integrating tools and techniques from structural graph theory into tools for scalable network analysis.

Featured activities below are intended to be a representative snapshot of recent activities. Please see CV for complete lists.
