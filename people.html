Principal Investigator

Dr. Blair D. Sullivan

Dr. Sullivan earned her Ph.D. in Mathematics from Princeton University, and Bachelors degrees in Computer Science and Applied Mathematics from the Georgia Institute of Technology. Prior to moving to NC State in Fall 2013, she was a Research Scientist in the Computer Science and Mathematics Division at Oak Ridge National Laboratory.

Want to Join Us?

Undergrad at NC State who loved CSC226 and CSC333? Know the basics of coding in C (or C++) and comfortable on a Unix command line? Send an email to Dr. Sullivan. Opportunities to participate in research for credit or stipend available.

Looking to do a PhD? Apply to the Computer Science Department at NC State! List Dr. Sullivan as a faculty member you're interested in working with, and I'll review your file during admissions. Warning: my students are expected to prove things.

We expect to have at least one postdoc opening in the next few months. Watch this space (and send an email letting us know of your interest and availability)!
