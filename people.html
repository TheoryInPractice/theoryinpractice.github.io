Want to Join Us?

Undergrad at NC State who loved CSC226 and CSC333? Know the basics of coding in Python and comfortable on a Unix command line? Knowledge of git version control and/or LaTeX document formatting are plusses. Send an email to Dr. Sullivan with subject line "Undergrad Research". Opportunities to participate in research for credit or hourly pay/stipend available. Fall 2016/Spring 2017 slots are filled - we should have openings in Summer/Fall 2017.

Looking to do a PhD? Apply to the Computer Science Department at NC State! List Dr. Sullivan as a faculty member you're interested in working with, and I'll review your file during admissions. Warning: my students are expected to prove things.
